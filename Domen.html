Domen

about

The Dome is a green oasis, where you get to enjoy music, art and culture.

The beautiful tree and glass dome is the home of an innovative cultural centre where sustainability is key. The unique architecture makes the dome shaped cultural centre stand out at the industrial harbour, and stepping inside the dome is an amazing experience.

Domen feels like an oasis of peace and focus surrounded by the sea
